Biography

I work toward equality of opportunity. My research shows that ostensibly poor life choices in adolescence may arise as an optimal response to early disadvantage. But I also find that adolescents are capable of exerting consious control over those choices and their future developmental pathway. My early research sought to understand intergenerational poverty; I now also work to change it.
